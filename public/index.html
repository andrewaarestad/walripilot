WalriPilot - The Full-Featured Aquatic Mammal Autopilot!

The Fighting Walrus development team is proud to bring you the ultimate open source UAM! (Unmanned Aquatic Mammal)

WalriPilot Platform Feature List

  • Mammal Control Control your aquatic mammal with an iPad or iPhone
  • Gyro stabilized flight mode enabling acrobatics (loops and barrel rolls)
  • GPS for position hold, waypoints and return-to-launch
  • Magnetometer for heading determination
